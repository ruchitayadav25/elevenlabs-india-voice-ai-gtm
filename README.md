## The Core Insight

India has 20+ major languages, 1000+ dialects, and a population that overwhelmingly prefers voice over text — especially in Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities.

But most AI voice products fail here. Not because of bad technology. Because they sound like they were trained somewhere else.

**The insight: Voice adoption in India won't be driven by capability. It'll be driven by trust — and trust starts with sounding like you belong.**

## The Strategy at a Glance

### Repositioning
| From | To |
|------|-----|
| "AI voice generation tool" | "Voice experiences that sound like your customer" |

### Target Segments

| Segment | Use Case | Core Problem Solved |
|---------|----------|---------------------|
| B2B SaaS & Startups | Customer support, onboarding | Robotic bots that users don't trust |
| EdTech Platforms | Multilingual teaching assistants | English-only platforms failing regional learners |
| Fintech | Regional language product explainers | Drop-offs when customers don't understand |
| D2C Brands | Personalized voice campaigns | Generic brand voice that feels like a billboard |

### Campaign: *"Sounds Like You"*

A campaign built around one simple idea — show people what it feels like to hear an AI that sounds like someone they actually know.

- **Language** — sounds local
- **Tone** — feels familiar
- **Context** — fits the moment
- **Intent** — earns trust

No product pitch. Just a before and after. The difference speaks for itself.

## Go-To-Market Channels

**LinkedIn (B2B)**
Real stories from startups that changed outcomes by changing their voice. Case studies written like human experiences, not ROI reports.

**Short-form Video**
Before vs after voice comparisons in regional languages. Designed to stop someone mid-scroll.

**India Landing Page**
Not a translated global page — a page built for India from the first word, with use cases rooted in Indian industries and languages.

**Community Seeding**
SaaSBOOMi, iSPIRT, NASSCOM, Razorpay Rize — trust networks that matter more than paid placement.

---

## Flagship Event: *"Voice AI for India — Beyond Translation"*

A closed-door roundtable for CMOs, Product Heads, and CX leaders.

No decks. No pitch.

We play two voices — one generic, one built for their user. The room decides which one they trust.

- **Format:** Closed-door roundtable
- **Audience:** 50–100 curated enterprise decision-makers
- **Hook:** Live demos in 5+ Indian languages
- **Follow-up:** Custom use case demos per industry

## What I Learned Building This

India isn't one market — it's thirty markets layered on top of each other. Any GTM strategy that treats it as one will fail.

The companies that win in Voice AI here won't be the ones with the best models. They'll be the ones that understood people first — their hesitations, their languages, their lives — and built trust before they built pipelines.

That's the kind of marketing I want to do.
