"Sounds Like You"

Not a product demo. A feeling. We show real people — in real cities, real industries, real languages — hearing an AI voice that finally sounds like someone they know. No narration. No pitch. Just the before and after, and a question: which one did you trust?

The four dimensions of relatability

Language

Hindi, Tamil, Marathi, Bengali — voice AI that starts where the user lives

Tone

Warm where it should be warm. Professional where it needs to be. Never just correct.

Context

A loan explanation sounds different than a lesson. The voice should know the difference.

Intent

Trust isn't built by information — it's built by how the information arrives.
